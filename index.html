Revolut hires Product Owners who own the roadmap, work hands-on with engineers, and partner with FinCrime, Legal, and Security every week. That is exactly the work I have been doing for the last five years.

Regulated fintech is my home

Banking, derivatives, AML/KYC, mortgage underwriting, BFSI document operations. I know what happens when an onboarding flow fails silently or a compliance flag is wrong — because I have been on the operations side of it.

Cross-functional by default

I have worked weekly with Engineering, Design, Compliance, FinCrime, Legal, and Sales. I write user stories with engineers in the morning and present roadmap decisions to enterprise CISOs and CROs in the afternoon.

ROI-based prioritisation

I prioritise on operational ROI, not feature wish-lists. 70% manual ops reduction. 45% compliance effort cut. +18% conversion uplift. 12% churn reduction. 34% faster time-to-market. Outcomes are how I argue trade-offs.

Bias to action, hands-on bootstrap

I have launched products from zero, shifted release cadence from monthly to weekly, and stood up new third-party integrations end-to-end. An 80% solution running in production beats a perfect deck every time.

Own the outcome, not the feature. The job is to move the metric customers and Compliance both feel.
